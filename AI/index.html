About the Game

Nano: the Gathering is a two-player deck-building card game starring Nano, a white Sealyham Terrier of extraordinary talent and questionable judgment. Inspired by Magic: the Gathering, players compete by deploying Character cards, spending Treats as resources, and unleashing Action cards to swing the game in their favor.

Build a deck around the Nap synergy for a patient defensive fortress, or stack Characters like Taco Nano and Death Star Nano for an overwhelming offensive juggernaut. Cards come in three types — Characters who battle on the field, Actions for instant effects, and Treats that generate the resources to play everything else.

Two ancient forces define the universe: Cheese and Chocolate. Every card, every strategy, and every deck echoes that eternal conflict.

⚡

Cheese

The golden force. Aggressive strategies, treat generation, and relentless forward pressure.

🌑

Chocolate

The dark force. Patience, board control, and the long game — waiting to dismantle everything.
